DE HEMELSE BLOEM

(The Divine Flower) — Documentary | Digital | 27'44 | DCP | 2022

The Divine Flower explores how the current generation of young women can break free from (learned) patterns of guilt and shame surrounding body image and sexuality, and learn to put their own physical pleasure and self-image first.

Director Tessa van der Riet
Producers Hannah Borchard & Eleonora Minervino
Cinematography Hugo van Roosmalen
Production Design Isa Soares
